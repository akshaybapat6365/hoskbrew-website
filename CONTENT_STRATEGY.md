**2. Knowledgeable & Authentic**

**3. Creative & Technical**

- Balances artistic vision with manufacturing precision

- "helping creatives bring their homebrew and retro-inspired ideas to life"

- Welcoming to newcomers, respectful to veterans

- "Buried Worlds. Explosive Enemies. And the Clock Is Melting."

## Writing Guidelines

### Do's

- Use game-world language and references when appropriate
- Balance technical accuracy with accessibility
- Use second person ("you", "your") to create personal connection
- Include specific technical details (platforms, specifications)
- Employ dramatic, urgent language for game releases and limited items
- Show passion for the craft and community

### Don'ts

- Be overly corporate or sterile
- Use generic marketing fluff without substance
- Assume technical knowledge (explain terms when first used)
- Be dismissive of any gaming niche or community
- Sacrifice authenticity for polish

### Vocabulary Preferences

**Use These Words:**

- homebrew, retro-inspired, bespoke
- craft, forge, build, create
- passion, community, play
- revive, restore, preserve
- cult classic, legendary, authentic

**Avoid These Words:**

- "synergy", "paradigm", "leverage" (corporate speak)
- "cheap", "budget" (implies low quality)
- "just", "simply" (minimizes complexity)
- Generic superlatives without backing ("best", "greatest")

3. **H3 (Subsections):** Specific, technical when needed
